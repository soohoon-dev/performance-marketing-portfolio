# GEN.TALK: Cultural Brand Activation Strategy

**Project Type**: Brand experience proposal (2019)  
**Client Context**: Genesis (Hyundai Motor America) - G70 launch  
**Target**: Premium Korean-American community (Los Angeles)  
**Concept**: TED-style knowledge-sharing platform

---

## üéØ Challenge

Position Genesis G70 as a **premium lifestyle brand** (not just "Korean car") within the influential Korean-American community.

**Strategic Insight**: LA Korean community (30s-50s professionals) seeks intellectual networking and mentorship, but no branded platform existed to serve this need.

---

## üí° Solution: GEN.TALK Knowledge Platform

### Event Concept

Premium offline series (3x/year) combining lecture, networking, and dining

**Theme**: "Paradigm Shift" - Inspiring immigrant community perspective change

**Venue**: CGV Genesis Hall (cultural hub)

### Speaker Curation Strategy

High-profile lineup representing Korean-American success:

- **Min Jin Lee**: Author of _Pachinko_ (Nobel nominee) - Identity & Literature
- **Min Kim**: CEO Open Bank - Business Growth & Giving Back
- **Dennis Hong**: Robotics leader - Technology Innovation
- **Social Impact**: KAGC civic leaders, filmmakers

### Brand Integration

G70 as lifestyle enabler (not hard-sell):

- Ambassador program with influencers
- "Photo with Genesis" experience zones
- Post-event digital community building

---

## üìä Strategic Framework

**Promotion**: Hyper-targeted digital (100% digital, no print)  
**Channels**: MissyUSA, Bay Area K Group, Amazon Seller communities

**Event Flow**:

1. Pre-Event: Photo call & dining (ice-breaking + brand exposure)
2. Keynote: Intellectual engagement (brand trust building)
3. Networking: Community building (relationship)
4. G70 Experience: Product interaction & lead generation
